What is chocolate?

Chocolate is derived from the bean of a coach plant. It is then roasted and cooked to make cocoa mass. At this stage it is still quite bitter, even more than a 80% dark chocolate bar! The next step is to add butter milk and sugar. Fun fact: White chocolate is not really chocolate, as it has no cocoa!

Sustainability

Our commitment to quality extends further than our signature chocolates. We value our producers as well. That is why we use only small batches of the best quality cocoa beans from the heart of Peru. We work with only local farms in the region that adhere to organic and biodynamic practices.
