What is chocolate?

Chocolate is derived from the bean of a coach plant. It is then roasted and cooked to make cocoa mass. At this stage it is still quite bitter, even more than a 80% dark chocolate bar! The next step is to add butter milk and sugar. Fun fact: White chocolate is not really chocolate, as it has no cocoa!
